About

One Weather is a web application that provides weather, astronomy, and sports news information based on your location or the city name you enter. Our goal is to provide accurate and up-to-date information to our users in an easy-to-use and visually appealing format.

What we do

One Weather uses Angular JS, HTML, and CSS for front-end development, and JSON Server for the back-end database. We also use OpenWeather API, Ninja API, and Astronomy API to fetch data from different sources. Our features include a login and register page, current and hourly weather, daily weather with sorting options, an astronomy view with sunrise/sunset times and moon phases, sports news for football, cricket, and golf, and unique facts cards with 10 random facts that change every time you refresh the website.
